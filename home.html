E-learning is a better wary of learning

With German Online, you can set your learning goals and choose from a variety of lessons from German for work to everyday German , to improve your grammar, speaking, pronunciation and more. Study using our interactive platform and connect in a global classroom of 10 students at times that suit you. Track your progress through tests and assessments and receive live feedback from your teachers. Our courses are best suited for students with a Pre-intermediate level of German or above. If you're not sure of your current level, don't worry - you can take a free level test with German Online.
